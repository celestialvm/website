What is it?

It's a Ubuntu instance, managed by Vagrant, hosting a bunch of pre-made and bespoke Ethereum-related tools.

What's the goal?

Primarily to allow anyone to run a local Ethereum node without doing much, and have a safe environment to manage their funds.

Who's behind it?

The same guy who made EtherAddressLookup.
